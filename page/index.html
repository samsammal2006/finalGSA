ABOUT US

At Get Skill Academy, we believe in providing quality education that is accessible to everyone. Our mission is to equip our students with the knowledge and skills required to excel in their chosen fields.

Our team of experienced instructors and comprehensive curriculum are designed to help you achieve your goals and succeed in your career.

Vision:

Get Skill Academy aims to provide a diversified and sustainable portfolio of skills through enhanced education and training programs for both domestic and international learners. Our vision centers around innovation, creativity, and empowerment.

Mission:

Our mission is to create an avenue that enables every learner or student to receive customized, high-quality skills that empower them to add value to their lives through sustained job creation and employment. We strive for timely program completion, high first-time pass rates for functional skills, and successful apprenticeship outcomes.

Core Values:

1. Impact: We focus on creating positive impacts on the planet, people, and prosperity.

2. Integrity: Trust and transparency are at the core of our operations.

3. Inclusion: We provide access to skilling for disadvantaged socio-economic groups, promoting equal opportunities.
